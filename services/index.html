Common questions

What is the difference between Model Build and the stage-specific packages?
Model Build is a general-purpose engagement that includes 8 hours of walkthrough support. The stage-specific packages (Pre-Seed through Series B) are scoped to the complexity your stage demands: Pre-Seed covers foundation assumptions and 18-month runway, while Series B includes multi-geography currency management, balance sheet integration, and M&A scenario tabs.
What does the Investor Materials Package include beyond a Model Build?
The Investor Materials Package bundles a full model build with pitch deck review and restructure, data room setup and population, a curated investor target list of 50 to 100 names, and a pre-meeting preparation brief per investor. It is the all-in-one option for founders who want every deliverable ready before their first meeting.
Do I need to commit before the call?
No. Every service starts with a free 30-minute strategy call. You describe your situation and goals, and we recommend the right service. There is no obligation to proceed.
Can I upgrade from one service to another?
Yes. If you start with a Readiness Audit and realise you need a full Model Build or Investor Materials Package, the audit fee is credited toward the larger engagement. Upgrades happen on every second or third engagement.
How long does each engagement take?
The Model + Video Session is a single 1-hour call. The Readiness Audit takes 2 to 3 hours with a report delivered within 48 hours. Model Build and stage-specific packages run 1 to 3 weeks. The Investor Materials Package runs 2 to 4 weeks. Fractional Advisory is ongoing for the duration of the raise, typically 2 to 4 months.
