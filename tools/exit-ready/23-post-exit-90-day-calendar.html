Post-Exit 90-Day Calendar

Week-by-week roadmap for your first three months after closing

Chapter 32-33

The 90 days following your business sale are critical. You have major decisions to make about wealth management, family communication, tax strategies, and personal transition. This structured calendar ensures you cover all essential actions during this high-stakes window, preventing costly oversights.

The post-exit 90-day plan balances immediate action items with longer-term life decisions. By following this structured calendar, you'll handle the technical requirements while also creating space for the emotional and personal adjustments that come with major life transitions after stepping away from your company.
