Pillar Guides for Startup Founders

Four complete primers covering the core operational disciplines for founders raising venture capital: financial modelling, fundraising, metrics, and financial operations. Each pillar links to every detailed article we have published on the topic.

Financial Modelling

Everything you need to build an investor-grade three-statement financial model, from unit economics and revenue drivers to scenario analysis, runway, and cap table integration. Start here if you have never built a model before or if your current model keeps breaking under due diligence.

Fundraising

The complete fundraising playbook: investor targeting, pitch deck structure, data rooms, term sheets, diligence, and closing. Start here if you are preparing for a Seed, Series A, or Series B and need a structured approach.

Startup Metrics

Which metrics investors actually care about at each stage, how to define them without gaming, and how to report them to boards and shareholders. Start here if you are preparing a monthly investor update or a board deck and do not know where to start.

Financial Operations

The operational side of startup finance: budgets, forecasts, invoicing, collections, tax, audits, and the monthly close. Start here if your company is past product-market fit and you need basic financial hygiene.
