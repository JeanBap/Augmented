About Yanni Papoutski

VP Finance and Strategy. Author of Raise Ready. Builder of financial models. Advisor to founders.

Professional Background

I am a finance operator and strategist who has spent the last decade helping founders navigate the intersection of capital raising, financial modeling, and unit economics. My experience spans multiple continents and funding stages, from pre-seed through Series B and beyond.

I have worked directly with companies backed by Creandum, Profounders, and B2Ventures. I have built financial models for Indeed Flex (Syft), Simplifii, and Haus. I have helped founders across the UK, US, and Dubai markets understand their numbers deeply enough to raise capital confidently and operate more decisively.

Across five funding rounds and seven venture capital firms, I have raised over $20 million in capital and been part of exits exceeding $100 million in total value. These are not theoretical numbers. They are the ground truth that informs every article I write and every piece of advice I give.

What I Do

Writing

I write about the intersection of finance, strategy, and startup building. 15 years of experience covering fundraising, financial modeling, unit economics, and founder insights. Every article is battle-tested across real fundraises and real companies.

Consulting

I work with founders 1-on-1 to build financial models, prepare for fundraising, and structure their cap tables. From intro calls to full model builds, I help founders translate their business into the language investors understand.

Fractional Finance

I provide fractional CFO and FP&A support for early-stage companies. AI-native but human-refined. I help founders stay operationally disciplined without the overhead of a full-time finance hire.

Teaching

Through the Raise Ready book and companion products, I distill 16 chapters of battle-tested knowledge into a playbook that covers the complete lifecycle of startup finance from pre-seed through exit.

For fractional finance support and ongoing strategy work, visit the Services page.
